What is MSchedule?


Are you sick of using WolverineAccess to set up your schedule at UofM? We think most people agree that WolverineAccess is not worth the millions that the University spent on it. That's why we created MSchedule. MSchedule is not only faster for checking the course catalog, but it also has a lot more useful features. And all of this is FREE. Read below to find out just what MSchedule can do for you.


Features


Everyone (no registration neccessary)
  • search the UofM course catalog
  • search our database of building locations
  • automatic schedule builder
  • visual schedule builder
  • view other people's schedules (except for those that are privacy-protected)
    •

Members-only (membership is free, you can register here)
  • save schedules that you build
  • save one of those schedules as your "real" schedule
  • find friends in your courses
  • rate professors and courses that you have taken
  • set up meetings with other members during common free times
  • add one-time obligations to your calendar to show when you're not available
    •
